About Graywater Studios

At Graywater Studios, we're not just game developers; we're dream weavers, story crafters, and digital architects. Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate gamers turned developers, we've grown from a small indie studio to a powerhouse of creativity in the heart of Robloxia.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm, creating games that not only entertain but inspire, challenge, and bring people together. With a team of over 150 talented individuals from diverse backgrounds, we're committed to fostering an inclusive environment where innovation thrives.

Did you know? Our office space features a life-size replica of Bloxenheimer's nuclear reactor, complete with a slide that leads directly to our brainstorming pool!

Our Team

Led by our visionary CEO, oshawottice, our team comprises industry veterans and fresh talent alike. From our award-winning art department to our AI behavior specialists, each member brings a unique perspective to our collaborative process.
